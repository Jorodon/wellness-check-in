Resources

Prelude

Your mental and physical health are both very important to your wellbeing, however we sometimes don't take our mental health as serious as we should. Below we have provided some resources to help guide you in getting help, which we strongly recommend viewing.

We have broken these resources into sections for easier viewing.

988 Lifeline

Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

If you have thoughts of self-harm, we highly recommend you to call 988 immediately. Your life matters.

988 Lifeline is a free mental health hotline available 24/7, 265 days a year. Their mission statement:

“At the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, we understand that life's challenges can sometimes be difficult. Whether you're facing mental health struggles, emotional distress, alcohol or drug use concerns, or just need someone to talk to, our caring counselors are here for you. You are not alone.”

General nonprofits for everyone

NAMI

National Alliance on Mental Illness

NAMI is a grassroots, nonprofit organization dedicated to helping those affected by mental illness. Their mission statement:

"NAMI provides advocacy, education, support and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by mental illness can build better lives."

View Website

MHA

Mental Health America

MHA is a leading non-profit organization dedicated to research and advocacy for mental health. Their mission statement:

“Mental Health America advances the mental health and well-being of all people living in the U.S. through public education, research, advocacy and policy, and direct service.”

View Website

Nonprofits dedicated to youth and young adults

Active Minds

Active Minds is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing support for youth and young adults to help them succeed in life. Their mission statement:

“Active Minds mobilizes youth and young adults to lead a transformative movement in mental health, forever changing how it is valued and prioritized in society. By equipping, connecting, and amplifying the collective and diverse voices of youth and young adults, we are building a movement of lasting champions who are improving mental health norms for all.”

View Website

JED

The Jed Foundation

JED is a nonprofit organization that provides resources for youth and young adults to learn about mental health and ways to manage it. Their mission statment:

“The Jed Foundation (JED) is a nonprofit that protects emotional health and prevents suicide for our nation's teens and young adults.”

View Website

Gatorwell

Health Promotion Services

Gatorwell is UF's free health service dedicated to educating UF students on health and mental wellbeing, offering services like wellness coaching, HIV testing, alcohol/drug support services, and presentations/workshops. Gatorwell is partnered with the Jed Foundation. Their mission statement:

“Empowering UF students to build their own path toward lifelong wellbeing through prevention, advocacy, and sustainable behavior change.”

View Website

Additional Resources

Help Guide

Help Guide is a nonprofit organization dedicated to researching and providing support for mental health issues. It aims to inform people of mental health and teach skills and strategies to manage and improve it. Help Guide is partnered with Harvard Health Publishing. Their mission statement:

“We empower you with the knowledge and skills you need to strengthen your mental health & well-being.”

View Website

Sleep Foundation

By Sleep Doctor

Sleep Foundation is a subsidiary of Sleep Doctor, and conducts research on sleep patterns and health to provide resources for those who wish to improve their sleep. The Sleep Doctor brand also sells many sleep accessories and supplements designed to aid in sleep. From their "About Us" page:

"As part of the Sleep Doctor brand, SleepFoundation.org is driven by one mission: to help you sleep better, every night. Our team of researchers, writers, and sleep specialists delivers science-backed insights, expert-tested product reviews, and the latest news in sleep health, so you can make informed decisions and wake up ready for your day."
